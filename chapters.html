Chapters

Epiloque

Prologue is the very first chapter of Celeste. The chapter servers as a tutorial for walking and jumping. It is very calm with almost nothing to kill you. The level is placed under the main mountain on a parking lot where the story begins. There is mostly snow on the ground

Forsaken City

Forsaken City is the first chapter of Celeste. It introduces several core mechanics of the game, notably octadirectional dashes, red strawberries, red winged strawberries, and dash-refilling diamonds. Additionally, Theo is first introduced during this chapter.

Old Site

Old Site is the second chapter of Celeste. Most of it occurs during one of Madeline's dreams. It introduces Badeline for the first time, and also adds several new mechanics, including Dream Blocks, Seeded Strawberries, and Touch switches (also known as coins or shields).

Celestial Resort

Celestial Resort is the third chapter of Celeste. It occurs in the decrepit Celestial Resort Hotel. It introduces Mr. Oshiro. This chapter notably features a vast number of Dust Bunnies, also known as Sludge, which are the products of Mr. Oshiro's insecurities.

Golden Ridge

Golden Ridge is the fourth chapter of Celeste. It takes place on a windy cliff which scales Celeste Mountain.This chapter introduces several new elements to the game, including bouncy clouds and green bubbles; it also marks Granny's first appearance since the prologue.

Mirror Temple

Mirror Temple is the fifth chapter of Celeste. It introduces five new mechanics: Swap Blocks, Red Bubbles, Seeker, Seeker Barriers, and the Theo Crystal. In this chapter Theo gets stuck in a mirror and Madeline has to carry him around the end of chapter.

Reflection

Reflection is the sixth chapter of Celeste. It introduces five new mechanics: feathers, Kevin, Bumpers, Badeline as a boss, and Badeline moving blocks. Madeline gets into here after falling from the camp they built near Mirror Temple. It is located almost at the base of the mountain. Madeline also obtains a second dash at the end of the chapter.

TheSummit

The Summit is the seventh chapter of Celeste. The Summit is a step up in difficulty. The last part features flags that work like checkpoints. This chapter serves as the end of the main story. The Summit introduces five new mechanics: upwards and downwards wind and sideways springs.

Core

Core is the eighth chapter of Celeste. The chapter is locked behind a Crystal Heart Gate, requiring four Crystal Hearts to enter for the A-Side, 15 hearts for the B-Side, and 23 hearts for the C-Side. The main gimmick of the level is the ability of switching the level from the Ice state to Lava and back. Madeline also doesn't recharge her dash when landing.

Farewell

Farewell is both the final chapter of Celeste, as well as the longest and hardest. It features new mechanics such as 2-Dash Refills, Pufferfish and Jellyfish. Farewell introduces Wavedashes to the player. Unlike other chapters in Celeste, only the A side is present. The Bird becomes a central character and a gameplay mechanic.

This is a fanmade project that is in no way associated with Celeste or Extremely OK Games, Ltd.
