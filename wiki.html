Celeste is a two-dimensional platformer video game by Maddy Makes Games Inc., released in 2018. The player controls Madeline, who embarks on a journey to climb the mysterious Mount Celeste, uncovering herself throughout the trip. The game comprises 9 gameplay chapters with different mechanics and challenges. It has amassed considerable modding and speedrunning community.
